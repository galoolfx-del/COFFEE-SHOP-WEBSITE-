What we offer

Services beyond the counter

Private Events

After-hours buyouts and a private cupping bar for groups up to 30 — full menu or a custom tasting flight.

Office & Event Catering

Batch brew, cold brew kegs, and a mobile espresso cart for meetings, weddings, and offices.

Bean Subscriptions

Bi-weekly or monthly bags of whatever's freshest off the roaster, shipped the day after roasting.

Wholesale

We roast for a handful of other independent cafés and restaurants who want our beans on their bar.

Barista Training

A structured, hands-on program for cafés who want their team pulling shots to our own house standard.

Loyalty Program

Every tenth drink free, plus first access to seasonal releases before they hit the printed menu.

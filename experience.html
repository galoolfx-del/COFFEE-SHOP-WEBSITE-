A visit, start to finish

The experience

Ember & Oak changes shape through the day — here's what to expect depending on when you come in.

6:30am

The pour-over bar opens first

Before the espresso machine is even warmed up, the pour-over bar is running for the early crowd — V60 and Kalita, brewed to order.

8–10am

Morning rush at the espresso counter

Two baristas on bar, shots recalibrated for the day's humidity before the first ticket ever prints.

Midday

The community table fills up

Laptops out, slower pace — this is when the cold brew bar gets busiest.

Saturday, 10am

Public cupping sessions

A free weekly tasting of whatever's newest off the roaster, open to anyone who walks in.

Evenings

Acoustic sets, twice a month

Local musicians play the corner by the window — no cover, just buy a drink.

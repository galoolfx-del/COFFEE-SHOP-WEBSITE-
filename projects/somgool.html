Oak & Honey Latte

Local wildflower honey and a touch of smoked oak syrup, poured over a slow-pulled double shot. Our longest-running seasonal drink.

The Problem

Most "honey lattes" are just a pump of generic honey syrup — sweet, but flat, with nothing that couldn't be poured from a plastic bottle at any chain.

The Approach

We use raw wildflower honey from a beekeeper two towns over, whisked in warm rather than dumped in cold so it actually integrates instead of sinking. A house-made smoked oak syrup — steeped over lightly charred oak chips — adds a whisper of campfire warmth underneath.

The espresso is pulled slightly longer than our standard shot, so it can stand up to the honey without disappearing.

Tasting Notes

  • Raw wildflower honey, whisked in warm for full integration
  • House-made smoked oak syrup for a subtle campfire note
  • A slightly longer double shot to balance the sweetness
  • Available with oat or whole milk

Ingredients

Local wildflower honey, house oak syrup, espresso, and a choice of oat or whole milk — no artificial flavoring.

Availability

On the menu September through February; returns each fall as one of our most-requested seasonal drinks.
